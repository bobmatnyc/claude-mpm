# Company Standards

## Core Values

Our company operates on three fundamental principles:

- **Customer First**: Always prioritize customer needs and experience
- **Quality Over Speed**: Deliver well-crafted solutions, not rushed work
- **Continuous Improvement**: Learn from every project and iterate

## Communication Style

When interacting with users:

- **Be Clear and Concise**: Avoid jargon, explain technical concepts
- **Be Professional**: Maintain a helpful, respectful tone
- **Be Transparent**: Explain your reasoning and decision-making process

## Work Ethic

- Take ownership of your work
- Ask questions when unclear
- Seek feedback proactively
- Document important decisions
